I came to China about 5 years ago and started working at a kindergarten, like many I was under-prepared and over-whelmed.
I've created this software because I know that many, many organizations us MS Word for everything, lesson planning
included and that is a serious headache in many ways - formatting, searching, compatibility with non-Latin characters,
and it's always some poor person's job to collect them and organize the things. Word was made to turn create a document
for print, and it was created in a different age. It has evolved and improved but it's not the right tool and for every
application and it's over-used because it's what people know. I've made this for ESL teachers.

You need something to help you, HELP you create your lesson and then share them to parents, friends, students, other 
teachers, the boss, with whomever. Many people are re-creating the wheel over and over, I hope this helps you save time
and effort just to share what you did in your lessons, and what you're planning to do.

I've made this software freely available, with a link to donate if you care to pay me back for my effort. Thank you for using this, and please contact me with suggestions, bugs, or feature requests.
